I am Raghuram Manikandan, an Indian musician, vocalist, and a YouTuber. I have a deep inclination towards spirituality. I am immensely influenced by Sanatana Dharma.

I am in 12th standard now and under guidence of my gurus and my mentor Dr. Kuldeep Muralidhar Pai, best known as Kuldeep M.Pai I and my team have performed, recorded and successfully published most of popular Stutis of Shree.

We try to spread the awareness about our Sanatana Dhrma through these videos and will surely continue in future with your blessings. So I have my own YouTube channel and also some of my videos are also uploaded on "Kuldeep M Pai" channel. So visit our channel and experience the divinity.

Nama Ramayana | Raghuram Manikandan | Sree Ragam Music
Raghuram Manikandan (YouTube channel)

Jai Shri Rama!!!
Very excited and blessed to present Sri Valmiki's Nama Ramayana which describes Lord Rama's divine form, qualities and pastimes.
It consists of 108 verses which are divided into six chapters: Bala-kanda, Ayodhya-kanda, Aranya-kanda, Kishkindha-kanda, Sundara-kanda and Yuddha-kanda.
Apart from condensing the story, it helps the devotees to meditate on the different sterling aspects of Lord Rama.
